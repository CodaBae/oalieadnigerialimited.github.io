STRUCTURAL FABRICATION, PIPELINE CONSTRUCTION AND REPAIRS

Oaliead Nigeria Limited have the equipment, men and materials required to carry out any type of mechanical fabrication in any oil field location both on-shore and off-shore. We also have extensive experience in pipeline construction and repairs. We have constructed wellhead platform in several oil companies operating in the Nigerian delta region. Some of the mechanical fabrications and pipeline jobs handled includes but not limited to the following:

• Sectional replacement of well flow lines

• Construction of wellhead platform and hook-up.
