COMPANY OVERVIEW

OALIEAD NIGERIA LIMITED was incorporated in 2014 as a Private Limited Company with the aim of carrying out business in Engineering works and Procurement with specialization in the Oil and Gas industry. Oaliead Nigeria is proud of its expertise, efficiency and technical skill in Engineering Operations and Procurement. Due to strict adherence to safety regulations, the company has maintained an enviable position in the Oil and Gas Industry with integrity. The Company was incorporated with the primary objective of helping to develop and support the world quest for energy solution due to the increasing demand in the Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) in Nigeria Oil and Gas sector of our economy. The Company is wholly Nigerian and is poised to play a significant Role both in Nigeria and indeed in the world of oil and gas sectors and private environments. The Company has continued to grow and diversify its operations and is positioned to do business involving the Engineering expertise, Maintenance services, Procurement, Construction and Commissioning. Our scope covers Fabrication, Engineering, instrumentation, Procurement and Painting specification as mentioned in our services.
